About

I'm Putri Larasati Leksono, an Honours Computer Science student at UBC Okanagan graduating May 2026, specialising in machine learning, computer vision, and deep learning. Originally from Indonesia, I've had the rare opportunity to do research at a corporate R&D lab in Japan and an academic institute in Germany — before finishing my undergrad.
