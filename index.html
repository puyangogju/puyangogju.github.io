I'm Putri Larasati Leksono, an Honours Computer Science student at UBC Okanagan graduating May 2026, specialising in machine learning, computer vision, and deep learning. Originally from Indonesia, I've had the rare opportunity to do research at a corporate R&D lab in Japan and an academic institute in Germany — before finishing my undergrad.

Featured Engineering Project
Group Design Project · ITB · 2021–2022
Leachate Power Generator
Pembangkit Listrik Tenaga Lindi (PLTL) · Bandung Institute of Technology

Designed a bioelectrochemical system to generate electricity from landfill leachate — hazardous wastewater that forms as liquid percolates through solid waste. Rather than treating leachate as a waste problem, this system converts its mineral-rich chemistry into usable electrical output, addressing both environmental remediation and renewable energy simultaneously.

Produced complete technical schematics in AutoCAD and full 3D facility models in SketchUp. Designed for industrial scale: 507 reactor tubes, each built from resin with dual anode/cathode chambers connected by KNO₃ salt bridges.
